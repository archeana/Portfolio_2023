About me

Researcher aspiring to get into tech

As a researcher aspiring to transition into the tech industry, I am an archaeologist specializing in archaeobotany with a PhD in the field. After struggling to find suitable job opportunities, I embarked on a Project Management course where I discovered my passion for combining archaeobotany with technology. Eager to pursue this newfound interest, I delved into web development, quickly realizing its complexity and challenges.

My unique journey from researcher to web developer showcases my adaptability, persistence, and dedication to a career in technology. Join me as I combine my passion for archaeology and web development to create impactful digital experiences and contribute to innovative projects in the tech industry.
